What are NFTs?


NFTs, or Non-Fungible Tokens, are uniquie digital assests that represent ownership or proof of authenticity
of a particular item or piece of content. NFTs are normally built on blockchain technology, allowing for
secure transactions and proof of ownership without the need for intermediaries

LookOuts for NFTs

Minting NFTs

  • Here are the following types of NFT mints:

    1. Pro-Rata Mint -The community will have a period of time to send in their desired amount, if the max cap is at 1million ada, every individual will receive a small percentage of refund

    2. FSFC(First come first serve) Mint -When the mint opens up, the fastest transcation to go through will be able to get their NFTs

  • WhiteList. This is very important as you will be able to mint your NFTs early and for certain project the minting price will be cheaper

  • Try to use a Ethernet cable as this can increase your wifi speed. With FSFC mints, your transcation needs to be fast. Hence, ethernet cable is your best friend

  • Do your own research before minting any NFT projects as they will be rug-pulls(Creator taking the money and running) or you losing money

  • Not all influencer will have the correct read. Always do your research about the project that you are minting

Listing NFTs

  • To list your NFTs on the marketplace jpg.store is the best marketplace to list NFTs

  • Royalties. NFTs project will take up some royalties(small percentages of your listing amount) when you sell their NFTs. This is to help support the project

  • Offers. You can receive offers for your listing NFTs or you can offer to buy NFTs.This is like an auction

  • Floor= lowest price

  • Volume = total amount of ADA traded across the project
