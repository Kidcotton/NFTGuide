Treading Cryptocurrencies


What is
Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency is a digital,encrypted, and decentralized medium of exchange. Different from the US Dollar,there
is no authority that manages and maintains the value of a cryptocurrecy. As an alternative,
these tasks are broadly given among a cryptocurrecy's users via the internet.
